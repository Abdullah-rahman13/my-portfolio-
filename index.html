About Me

A lens shaped
by lived stories

I'm Abdullah Rahman — a documentary and fine-art photographer with over a decade of experience navigating the intersections of light, culture, and human emotion. My work is rooted in the belief that every frame is a conversation.

From the labyrinthine lanes of Old Dhaka to remote riverine communities, I seek images that carry weight — photographs that don't just depict a moment but hold it. My approach blends journalistic precision with painterly composition.

My work has been exhibited across South Asia, published in international editorial outlets, and recognised in several documentary photography awards. I also offer one-on-one workshops and mentorship for emerging photographers.
